About SOR Rates

Schedule of Rates (SOR) are standardized rate lists published by state governments for public works. Each state has its own SOR with different rates for materials, labor, and construction activities.

  • Your selected state's rates will be used in all estimates
  • You can change this setting at any time
  • Different modules can use different state rates if needed
